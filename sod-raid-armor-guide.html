Raid armor progression at a glance

1–2

Tiers 1 & 2 — No visible armor

Oceangreen Hills / Bloody Kithicor

No visible armor drops from Tier 1 or Tier 2 raid content.

3

Tier 3 — Eon Forged (standalone loot)

Field of Scale raids

Looted directly from raid bosses. No crafting required. These pieces cannot be upgraded and are not used in any combines. Comes in Plate, Chain, Cloth, and Leather.

4

Tier 4 — Concordant / Discordant (crafted)

Earth & Korafax raids

Loot a Primeval essence from raid bosses. Buy a Mutating Tinea + your class's Polymorphic Cocoon from Zebuxoruk. Combine to craft class-specific armor.

5

Tier 5 — Vested / Balanced / Chaotic (crafted)

Tower of Discord raids

Loot a Coeval essence from Tower of Discord events. Combine with your Tier 4 armor in a Temporal Molting Cell. Result depends on the color combination.

5.5

Tier 5.5 — Ordained / Tarnished / Tainted / Inflicted

Tower of Discord armor + group zone named drops

Loot an Eternal essence from named mobs in Tier 5 group zones. Combine with your Tower of Discord armor in a Temporal Molting Cell for the highest tier of visible raid armor.

Key concept: All SoD crafted armor is determined by the total count of blue (Luminessence) vs. orange (Incandessence) essences used across all tiers. Order doesn't matter — only the final ratio. Track your colors as you go.

Tier 3 — Field of Scale Raid Armor

Looted armor — no crafting

These pieces drop directly from raid events. They are standalone gear and cannot be upgraded or used in further combines. They come in Eon Forged Plate, Eon Linked Chain, Eon Woven Cloth, and Eon Hardened Leather.

Tier 3 raid armor is a gear check stepping stone. Once you have access to Tier 4 raid content, shift focus to crafting Concordant / Discordant armor using Primeval essences.

Tier 4 — Earth & Korafax Raid Armor

Step 1 — Loot your essence

Primeval Luminessences (blue) and Primeval Incandessences (orange) drop from these raid events:

Raid eventSlots
Showdown at the Crystal CoreWrist, Hands

Rathe Council Chambers: Rallos ReturnsHead, Feet
The Fall of ToskirakkHead, Feet, Arms, Legs
Korafax, Home of the Riders (3 events)Arms, Legs, Chest
Step 2 — Buy components from Zebuxoruk
A
Mutating Tinea — must match your armor slot. Wrist/Hands available in all Void versions; Feet/Head in B+; Arms/Legs in C+; Chest in D+.
B
Polymorphic Cocoon — class-specific crafting container. Available in all versions of The Void.
Free shortcut: Tavid Dennant in the Guild Lobby gives up to 16 containers free. Say "Cocoon" to get your class Polymorphic Cocoon at no cost.
Step 3 — Combine

Place Essence + Tinea inside the Polymorphic Cocoon and combine:

Essence usedResult
Primeval Luminessence (blue) → Concordant armor
Primeval Incandessence (orange) → Discordant armor
The stat difference between Concordant and Discordant is minimal (mainly mod2 stats). Pick whichever essence you loot — it primarily determines your path when upgrading to Tier 5.

Tier 5 — Tower of Discord Raid Armor

What you need
1
Your Tier 4 Concordant or Discordant piece from the Earth / Korafax step.
2
Coeval Luminessence (blue) or Coeval Incandessence (orange) — drops from any of the 7 events in the Tower of Discord.
3
Temporal Molting Cell — purchased from Zebuxoruk in The Void E, F, or G. Also free from Tavid Dennant (say "Molting").
Combine chart

Place Tier 4 armor + Coeval Essence inside the Temporal Molting Cell:

Tier 4 armor+ Essence= Result
Concordant Luminessence → Vested
Discordant Incandessence → Chaotic
Concordant Incandessence → Balanced
Discordant Luminessence → Balanced
Stat differences between Vested, Balanced, and Chaotic are minimal. Your choice here determines what final Tier 5.5 armor you can craft in the next step.

Tier 5.5 — Further Upgrade with Eternal Essences

What you need
1
Your Tower of Discord armor (Vested, Balanced, or Chaotic) from Tier 5.
2
Eternal Luminessence (blue) or Eternal Incandessence (orange) — looted from named mobs in Tier 5 group zones: Old Bloodfields, City of Dranik, or Korafax instances.
3
Temporal Molting Cell — from Zebuxoruk (Void E+) or free from Tavid Dennant (say "Molting").
Combine chart

Place Tower of Discord armor + Eternal Essence inside the Temporal Molting Cell:

ToD armor+ Essence= Final resultTotal ratio
Vested Eternal Luminessence → Ordained 3 blue, 0 orange
Vested Eternal Incandessence → Tarnished 2 blue, 1 orange
Balanced Eternal Luminessence → Tarnished 2 blue, 1 orange
Balanced Eternal Incandessence → Tainted 1 blue, 2 orange
Chaotic Eternal Luminessence → Tainted 1 blue, 2 orange
Chaotic Eternal Incandessence → Inflicted 0 blue, 3 orange
The rule: The final armor name is determined by the total count of blue vs. orange essences used across all three tiers of combining. Order does not matter — only the ratio.

Tips, Tools & Essence Management

Decoding essence names

Every essence name has three words, each telling you something specific:

Word positionMeaningValues
1st word Armor slot Distorted = Arms/Legs  ·  Fractured = Wrist/Hands  ·  Phased = Chest  ·  Warped = Head/Feet
2nd word Tier / source Seminal = group T3  ·  Medial = group T4  ·  Eternal = group T5  ·  Primeval = raid T4  ·  Coeval = raid T5
3rd word Color / type Luminessence = Blue  ·  Incandessence = Orange
Free containers — Tavid Dennant (Guild Lobby)

Visit Tavid Dennant and say these keywords to receive up to 16 free containers:

Say thisYou receiveUsed for
"Cocoon"Polymorphic Cocoon (class-specific)Crafting Tier 3 / raid T4 armor
"Cell"Temporal Polymorphic CellConverting blue ↔ orange essence
"Molting"Temporal Molting CellUpgrading armor through Tiers 4–5
"Luminescent"Luminescent Culling VortexRemoving the orange essence from armor
"Incandescent"Incandescent Culling VortexRemoving the blue essence from armor
⚠ The free Culling Vortex versions cannot downgrade essences to a lower tier — they only remove them. To downgrade a tier, buy the purchased version from Zebuxoruk and combine with a single Chronobine.
Swapping essence color

Got the wrong color? A Temporal Polymorphic Cell (free from Tavid Dennant) converts either direction:

Blue Luminessence → Temporal Polymorphic Cell → Orange Incandessence
Orange Incandessence → Temporal Polymorphic Cell → Blue Luminessence
Extracting an essence from existing armor

Changed your mind? As of Void D, Culling Vortices let you pull an essence back out:

ContainerRemovesReturns
Luminescent Culling Vortex The orange essence (naming is backward — trust it) Prior-tier armor + orange essence
Incandescent Culling Vortex The blue essence (also backward) Prior-tier armor + blue essence
Example: You have Tarnished armor (2 blue, 1 orange). Place it in a Luminescent Culling Vortex → receive Vested Tier 5 armor (2 blue) + your orange Tier 5.5 essence back. Convert that orange to blue using a Temporal Polymorphic Cell, then re-combine to produce Ordained.
SoF Prismatic armor shortcut

If you have a Secrets of Faydwer Tier 4 Prismatic piece — Chromasteel (plate), Chromalinked (chain), Kaleidosilk (silk), or Chromahide (leather) — you can skip Tier 3 entirely and combine it directly with a Medial essence using a Prismatic Mutation Nexus (purchased from Zebuxoruk, Void E+) to produce Tier 4 armor.
