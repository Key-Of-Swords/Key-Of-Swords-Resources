Strategy

KOS Raid Strategy

  1. Trash clear first. Clear all trash, move south killing everything before positioning the raid in the main room.
  2. Pull Queen to center of room. DPS begins.
  3. Smoldering Ash emote — entire raid runs out of melee range (AE Range 80) of Queen immediately. Return when safe.
  4. Wave of Infestation emote — marked player steps away from the raid. Curers: Group Corruption Cure / Radiant Cure immediately. Do NOT let it go uncured — adds cascade until cured.
  5. Enchanters: mez all spawned adds. Mezzable, extra weak to fire. Prioritize curing over killing adds to stop the chain.
  6. Full burn on the Queen. Then kill remaining adds.
  7. When Queen dies, chest appears but adds do NOT despawn. Kill remaining adds before looting.

Strategy

KOS Raid Strategy

  1. Stage at OGH at the Blackburrow entrance. Do not zone in until full raid is ready and buffed.
  2. Zone in — DO NOT MOVE FORWARD until everyone is in. When ready, move slightly forward. Trash mobs run away; only the Wrext Mal Martyr remains. Do NOT aggro/kill the martyr yet.
  3. Split raid into 8 groups (one mezzer in each group) and position each at a Totem location (use map coords above). SUSPEND ALL PETS AND NO DOTS.
  4. (> 9 in camp, including pets & swarm pets) you will get an extra set of 3 mobs.
  5. Once all 8 groups in position — kill the Wrext Mal Martyr. Once aggro'd, you have five minutes to kill it to initiate the event. Once killed a totem, 2 gnarls, and 1 "a Wrext Mal mundunugu" will spawn at each location. If too slow, martyr suicides and mobs spawn anyway.
  6. Per camp: Kill 2 Gnarls → bring Mundunugu to 20%, then to 10% and hold.
  7. On call: kill ALL 8 Mundunugu within 1 minute. If all of the Mundunugu are not killed, the camps all respawn.
  8. Regroup. Rottrued the Twisted spawns far east. DO NOT SLOW HIM. Doing so will power up Rottrued significantly. Tank + burn to kill.
  9. After Rottrued dies, head east to "a hidden agent." Hail it to spawn chest. Invisible flag granted on Rottrued death.

Strategy

KOS Raid Strategy

  1. Stage near zone-in. Do NOT move west until raid is fully buffed and ready. Moving west triggers the event and the 8-minute timer.
  2. Phase 1 — Balance Three Kyvs: Coordinate DPS to keep all three within 5% HP. At 75/50/25% they despawn and respawn in new positions. Watch Rasven (AE rampage) and Ythalym (flurry). Kill all 3 Kyvs before 8-minute mark. Assign certain classes (rogues, rangers, etc.) to equalize DPS.
  3. Phase 2 — Priest Camps: Direction emote fires (SW/NW/NE/SE). Assign Knight groups to each direction. Knight group needs to immediately aggro their assigned priest when they spawn to stop life drain on NPCs. The rest of the raid will move to whatever Priest is active. 30% Phase — Discordant Detritus and Discordant Doom (CRITICAL)

At 30%: Brekt targets up to a dozen players simultaneously with either Detritus or Doom

Strategy

KOS Raid Strategy

  1. Setup: Spell haste on all curers. MGB CR chain queued. Totems placed. All DPS buffs running. DA knight sets rampage at pull.

  3. Shadow emote: player at that location moves away immediately — 15k DD + 8k DoT lingers at the spot.
  4. Silence emote: all casters/melee back away from Brekt — silence aura surrounds him.

  6. Save all major burns for 30%. Hold Bloodlust, AA burns, everything — call at 30%.
