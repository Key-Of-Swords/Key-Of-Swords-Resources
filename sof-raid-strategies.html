Strategy

Raid Leader Strategy

  1. Vesthun Wolfpaw first: DPS Vesthun down. When he hits 75%, 50%, or 25% — raid off boss, kill the add wave (wolfcallers first, then worgs), then return to Vesthun. Vesthun's DPS escalates every 30 seconds so kill him efficiently. Adds do not despawn on his death.
  2. Clear Galrin & Wilnok's room before pulling them. Pull both brothers back to the same room where Vesthun was killed.
  3. Balance Galrin & Wilnok 5% at a time. Both must stay within 5% HP of each other at all times — if they diverge, both regen to 100%. DPS a few percent, check balance, continue.
  4. When adds spawn during the Boneshard fight — raid off boss, kill Dominated spirits first, then Relentless haunts, then return to the brothers.
  5. When both brothers are dead, move to the chest room and kill the remaining adds before looting.

Strategy

Raid Leader Strategy

  1. Zone in: Plow or invis to the Crystal Room. Approach Crazok to trigger the event. Kill all Bloodmoon channelers one at a time (non-aggro until attacked).
  2. Ralkor + Vesthun activate. MT on Ralkor — Warrior may want to use defensive. Keep Ralkor above 90% until Vesthun is dead. OT (SK) picks up Vesthun immediately.
  3. No DoTs on Ralkor throughout the fight.
  4. Cure Ralkor's Crippling off the MT — 36 curse counters, snare/slow/4k DoT/spell damage reducer.
  5. At Vesthun 50% — Worgcaller and worgs spawn. Mez worgs. Knight picks up Worgcaller. Kill Vesthun → Worgcaller → worgs in that order.
  6. DPS Ralkor to 90% — Galrin and Wilnok spawn. Tank them adjacent. Balance within 5% at a time. Kill Dominated spirits first, then Relentless haunts.
  7. DPS Ralkor to 75% — Malkazor and Balvik spawn. Kill Malkazor first, then Balvik. Move Balvik away from all bosses to keep heal aura clear.
  8. DPS Ralkor to 60% — Grendol spawns. Kill him quickly, then continue.
  9. DPS Ralkor to 40% — Harlos and Korvak spawn. Kill Harlos first (scaling AE aura), then Korvak. Raid avoids all three auras while either is alive.
  10. DPS Ralkor to 20% — Crazok spawns. Warrior picks up Crazok. Pop burns. Cure Crazok's Bite immediately on emote — 30-second Charm timer. Kill Ralkor first, then Crazok.
  11. Chest spawns on South side of the room when Ralkor dies. Remaining adds do not despawn.

Golem Phase — Triggered 13 Times (92% → 15%)
⚡
Mechanic: Starting at 92% health and repeating roughly every 5% down to 15%, Deepscar stops attacking, runs into a random side room, and spawns a Crystalline Golem. Aggro resets each time this occurs. This happens at approximately: 92%, 87%, 82%, 77%, 72%, 68%, 51%, 42%, 36%, 31%, 25%, 20%, 15% — 13 total spawns. Which golem type spawns is entirely random. Golems do not despawn.

Obsidian Golem

Continuously spawns Obsidian Crystal Golemite adds until the golem itself is killed.

Golemite adds can be mezzed.

Vermillion Golem

Spawns an Ember Mass with a 1,000 DoT aura. Stay out of the aura.

Adds may explode on death — be cautious on kills.

Viridian Golem

Spawns a Zephyr Vortex with a 2,000 DD spin-stun aura.

Avoid the vortex aura — it knocks back and stuns.

Saffron Golem

Chain-casts a 6,000 DD spell that hits anyone not in melee range.

⚠ Everyone must hug Saffron at melee range or die.

Cerulean Golem

Spawns a prison on players that can mez them or drain mana.

Prison can only be broken by players who are outside the trap.

Amethyst Golem

Has AE Rampage and an aura that increases incoming spell damage.

Casters pull back during this golem — spike risk is high.

Minotaur Adds Phase — Begins at 50%

Kirkoten Adds — Spawn Every ~60 Seconds

At 50% health, the West Room begins spawning one each of:

  • Kirkoten Arcanist (caster)
  • Kirkoten Oracle (caster/support)
  • Kirkoten Champion (melee)

These adds have low health, are immune to mez, do not despawn, and continue spawning until Deepscar is dead. Kill them promptly each wave.

Strategy

Raid Leader Strategy

  1. Main Tank holds Deepscar — tank stays on the boss at all times, even during golem phases. The raid follows Deepscar as he moves between rooms.
  2. Full raid stays on Deepscar — do not peel off the boss unless a golem spawns.
  3. On golem spawn — the Add Team breaks off and kills the golem (yellow con) first. After the golem is down, clean up any remaining add-spawns (white cons). Tank remains on Deepscar throughout.
  4. After golem is dead — the Add Team returns to Deepscar immediately. Rinse and repeat for all 13 golem phases.
  5. At 50% — Add Team adds Kirkoten adds to their kill priority. Kill minotaur waves (white cons) as they spawn. Stay ahead of the pile-up.
  6. Rain of Boulders — if you receive the emote with your name, immediately run at least 40 units away from the raid. Do not run toward other players.
  7. Saffron Golem — when Saffron spawns, everyone moves to melee range. No exceptions. 6k DD chain cast will kill players at range.
  8. Chest — spawns in the West Room after the kill.

  1. Everyone shrinks before entering. Ensure all raiders have their Igneous Crystalline Ember from the Fire Wing.
  2. Raid holds 200 range from the room until after the first Icy Breath fires.
  3. Paladin DA in and pulls Zeixshi`Kar to the North/Left under the table position.
  4. All tanks build aggro. Give tanks 10–15 seconds to establish before melee engages. Tanks are slowed by Icy Breath — melee watch aggro carefully and do not flip the boss.
  5. Raid moves in to South/Right under the table. Tank only in front of the mob.
  6. Icy Claw: Knocks the tank off aggro via FD + hate reduction. Quick tank swap — next tank picks up immediately. Give new tank 10–15 seconds to build aggro before melee ramps back up.
  7. Freezing Strike emote: When the emote fires, anyone turned into a Golem must be cured immediately by 3 different people clicking Igneous Crystalline Ember on them. Cure Golems when Icy Breath is cast. Tank swap while Golem tank is being cured.
  8. Icy Breath (PBAE 200 range): Tanks move out to AE taunt briefly, then back under the table. Try to RC the 30-curse snare component when group is hit.
  9. DPS Zeixshi`Kar until dead.
📋
Golem cure checklist: 3 different people with Igneous Crystalline Ember must each click on the Golem — one click does not cure. Coordinate who clicks to avoid 3 people from the same group all clicking without anyone else covering. Have designated curers assigned before the pull.

Hatchery Sequence

  • Four drake egg tenders (unattackable) path into the two wyvern rooms (2 per room) and hatch the eggs there.
  • Brood Mother (unattackable during this phase) walks around the hatchery hatching her own eggs — six types of young dragons: blue, gold, black, white, green, red. No special abilities.
  • These hatchery drakes have low HP but various AEs/auras: snares, DoTs, minor mana drain, spell slows, and ~8,000 DD.

  • Tunnel groups kill egg-tender adds before they path into Brood Mother's room.
  • All kills must happen out of Brood Mother's line of sight.
  • Remaining raid kills Brood Mother's own hatchery drakes until she becomes active.
